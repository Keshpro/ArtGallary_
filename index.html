I am Aggrani Karunarathna, a 22-year-old emerging artist based in Christchurch. My work is a fusion of bold colors, intricate patterns, and animal symbolism.

Specializing in vibrant, hand-drawn oil pastel illustrations, I explore the connection between nature's wild beauty and contemporary decorative art. Every piece is a story of patience, joy, and emotion brought to life on canvas.
